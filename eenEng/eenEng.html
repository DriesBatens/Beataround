Who?


Beat - A - Round



What?


A fictitious record label.



Where?


Beat - A - Round is established by graphic design students from AP in antwerp.



Why?


The genre we worked on was 'Eurodance', probably not a well-known concept for everyone. That is exactly what we want to change! Our genre must return a general concept and not be tucked away under the term '90's music’. Creating a wide public is one of our main goals. The music isn’t only enjoyable for the older generation, but the youngsters will also be able to dance on these catchy beats.
