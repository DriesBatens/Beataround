Bibi C

Story

Her life as a model is difficult to combine with her career as a singer. As a model, she must get up early, while her performing shows on the weekend often go on until the break of dawn. She doesn’t care what other people say and she likes to judge other people by their appearance. She’s a piece of work, that woman.

Goals

Bibi C likes to combine the dancing and the singing, she enjoys shining in the spotlight. A variety of colorful hairstyles will contribute to her mission; being known all over the world.

Duo

Story

This duo sees life through rose colored glasses. They like to be special and show that in the way they dress. In the week, they spend a lot of time running their business, but in the weekends they will most definitely go wild and they are in their element.

Goals

They want to improve their pub and evolve it into a real dance club, where they perform from time to time. It should be available for everyone. Their motto is that everyone should have fun in life.

Dj Dicious

Story

DJ Dicious has a great passion for creating music. In the daily life, he spends a lot of time mixing and matching the right beats. He sees life also very colorful, you can see this by his fancy tracksuit. His motto: 'Going comfortable through life.'

Goals

Creating an atmosphere where everyone feels comfy. The music he plays must be accessible for everyone, but most of all it must be danceable. The one who can’t dance on his music, is yet to be born.

TanzActiv

Story

A comic group, who fit perfectly on each other. The men take care of the beats and the woman takes care of the vocal parts in the show. They traveled all over the world together and learned something new in each country to add to their crazy performances.

Goals

Their goal is to be famous in as many countries as possible and evolve in terms of timeless music. Their music should remain known for years and the term "Eurodance" should too.
